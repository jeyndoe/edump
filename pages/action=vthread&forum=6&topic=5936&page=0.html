Можно через макрос:

literal MACRO quoted_text:VARARG
LOCAL local_text
.data
local_text db quoted_text,0
align 4
.code
EXITM <local_text>
ENDM

SADD MACRO quoted_text:VARARG
EXITM <ADDR literal(quoted_text)>
ENDM

invoke MessageBox, NULL, SADD('sdfdfs'), SADD('dfgsdfg'), MB_YESNO
