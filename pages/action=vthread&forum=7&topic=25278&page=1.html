Linux/ELF on i386, x86-64, ARM, PowerPCLinux/kernel on i386, x86-64 and ARM
Mach-O/ppc32, Mach-O/i386, Mach-O/x64 rtm32/PE tmt/adam PlayStation1/exe
watcom/le Win32/PE Win64/PE
