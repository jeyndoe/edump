TTmd5.dll adds MD5 signatures to tooltips.
CODE:[Copy to clipboard]TTmd5_install.exe is just a compiled batch file with a dll (TTmd5.dll) that:

1-Copies TTmd5.dll to %windir%

2-Calls regsvr32 and registers TTmd5.dll

3-Calls regedit and makes the following entries:

[HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT*shellex]
[HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT*shellex{00021500-0000-0000-C000-000000000046}]
@="{B20433A8-D083-11D4-993A-00D00912C440}"
[HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINESOFTWAREMicrosoftWindowsCurrentVersionShell ExtensionsApproved]
"{B20433A8-D083-11D4-993A-00D00912C440}"="TTmd5 Info View"

If you don't like the results just remove TTmd5.dll from your %windir%.
