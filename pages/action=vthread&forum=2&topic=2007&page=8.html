ты про это..:

here is a small bug in the dll initilization section:
Code:

024D1000 >/$ 55 PUSH EBP
024D1001 |. 8BEC MOV EBP,ESP
024D1003 |. 60 PUSHAD
024D1004 |. FF75 08 PUSH DWORD PTR SS:[EBP+8]
024D1007 |. 8F05 E5454D02 POP DWORD PTR DS:[24D45E5]
024D100D |. 837D 0C 01 CMP DWORD PTR SS:[EBP+C],1
024D1011 |. 75 05 JNZ SHORT advanced.024D1018
024D1013 |. E8 0A000000 CALL advanced.024D1022
024D1018 B8 01000000 MOV EAX,1
024D101D 61 POPAD
024D101E |. C9 LEAVE
024D101F \. C2 0C00 RETN 0C


Should be
Code:

024D1000 >/$ 55 PUSH EBP
024D1001 |. 8BEC MOV EBP,ESP
024D1003 |. 60 PUSHAD
024D1004 |. FF75 08 PUSH DWORD PTR SS:[EBP+8]
024D1007 |. 8F05 E5454D02 POP DWORD PTR DS:[24D45E5]
024D100D |. 837D 0C 01 CMP DWORD PTR SS:[EBP+C],1
024D1011 |. 75 05 JNZ SHORT advanced.024D1018
024D1013 |. E8 0A000000 CALL advanced.024D1022
024D1018 61 POPAD
024D1019 B8 01000000 MOV EAX,1
024D101E |. C9 LEAVE
024D101F \. C2 0C00 RETN 0C


Otherwise the POPAD will overwrite EAX. This could sometimes cause loadlibrary to fail depending on what was in EAX before-hand.
