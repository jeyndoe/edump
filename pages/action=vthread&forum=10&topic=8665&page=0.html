iNNos ?????:
What mean exactly that ? didnt understood this ,like what mean "you can poke this bytes" ?

Expression "you can poke this bytes" means you must simply change these bytes to 90.
For this goal you can use any HEX-editor or debugger OllyDbg. Just find the signature "6A 00 FF 15 04 10 40 00" and change each byte to 90.
Opcode "90" equals to command NOP - "empty" command, which force the processor do no operations.
